Bio:

Matt Blanco is a designer who uses technology and visualization as a means to rethink our perceptions of information. While studying a combination of computer science and graphic design he combines the two disciplines in a way to explore new representations for information. All to make the world around us slightly easier to understand.

Outside of his academic and career work, he is an avid adventurer taking time to explore different corners of the natural world through multi-week long backpacking trips. All captured on 35mm film photography.
