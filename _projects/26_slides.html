In 26 Slides

Project Type: Data Physicalization

Team
  • Matthew Blanco
  • Ryan Webber
  • Christina TRan

Using archival subway ridership data from 1920-1945, In 26 Slides tells the story of how the MTA became as we know it today. The work was showcased at the New York Transit Museum during The Transit Operas show.
