eyeWatch

Project Type: Speculative Service Design

Background

We are all aware our data is being collected. From Google, Meta, Apple, Amazon, you name it. Yet many people aren't aware of what kind of data is collected, and especially what it can do. eyeWatch imagines a speculative service where our collected data is put front and center. The service partners with big data companies to aggregate user data into a front-facing app providing an analysis of a user's information, while offering a series of sponsored recommendations to alter user behavior. A single design intervention cannot make people care about their digital data, but an intervention, of an absurd service, can begin to shed a light and help make people understand their data better.
